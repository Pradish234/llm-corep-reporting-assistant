## Section 2: Common Equity Tier 1 Capital

### 2.1 Definition of CET1
Common Equity Tier 1 capital shall consist of capital instruments and related share premium accounts, retained earnings, accumulated other comprehensive income, and other disclosed reserves, as adjusted by applicable regulatory deductions.

### 2.3 Eligibility Criteria
Institutions shall include in CET1 capital only those instruments that meet the eligibility criteria set out in the Own Funds Part of the PRA Rulebook.

---

## Section 3: Tier 1 Capital

### 3.1 Composition of Tier 1 Capital
Tier 1 capital shall consist of the sum of Common Equity Tier 1 capital and Additional Tier 1 capital.

---

## Section 4: Total Own Funds

### 4.1 Definition of Total Own Funds
Total own funds shall be the sum of Tier 1 capital and Tier 2 capital, subject to applicable limits and adjustments.
