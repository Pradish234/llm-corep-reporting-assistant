# COREP Instructions – Template C 01.00 (Own Funds)

## General Guidance
Template C 01.00 shall be used to report own funds in accordance with the applicable regulatory framework.

---

## Row 010 – Common Equity Tier 1 Capital
Institutions shall report the total amount of Common Equity Tier 1 capital as defined in the applicable Own Funds regulation.

---

## Row 020 – Tier 1 Capital
Institutions shall report Tier 1 capital as the sum of Common Equity Tier 1 capital and Additional Tier 1 capital.

---

## Row 030 – Total Own Funds
Institutions shall report total own funds as the sum of Tier 1 and Tier 2 capital, subject to regulatory adjustments.
